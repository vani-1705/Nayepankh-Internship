📖 Our Story

About NayePankh Foundation

A youth-led movement born during adversity, growing into a force for positive change.

Our Journey

NayePankh Foundation was born during the COVID-19 pandemic in 2021, when students and young volunteers in Kanpur came together to help families who had lost their income. What started as informal help quickly became a registered NGO — now holding UP Government registration with 12A and 80G certifications, and operating across 25+ cities in India.

🎯 Our Vision

A society where no child is denied education, no family sleeps hungry, and every person is treated with dignity.

🚀 Our Mission

Uplift underprivileged communities through education, hunger relief, clothing, health awareness and women's empowerment.

🌟 Our Goal

Expand city by city, build a strong volunteer base, and reach 10 lakh lives by 2027 with lasting support.

🛠️ What We Do

Our Works & Programs

Meaningful action across education, health, hunger and welfare.

Education Support

We help children from low-income families with books, basic supplies and guidance so that money is never the reason a child misses school.

Hunger Relief

Regular food distribution drives for families facing food insecurity, especially during emergencies and difficult seasons.

Women's Empowerment

Awareness sessions on menstrual health, distribution of sanitary napkins, and personal hygiene education for women and girls.

Clothing Distribution

Collecting gently used clothes from donors and distributing them to families and individuals who need warm or basic clothing.

Animal Welfare

Feeding stray animals in our localities and supporting basic animal welfare activities with the help of our volunteers.

Health Awareness

Simple health and hygiene awareness drives to help communities build better everyday habits and access basic healthcare knowledge.

📊 Impact of NayePankh Foundation

The Impact We've Made

Since 2021, every number here is a life touched.

🎓 Educational Impact

NayePankh Foundation has helped students gain better access to learning opportunities, educational resources, and guidance. Its initiatives encourage academic growth and support underprivileged children in pursuing their education.

🌱 Environmental Impact

Through tree plantation drives, cleanliness campaigns, and environmental awareness programs, the foundation has promoted sustainable practices and encouraged communities to protect and preserve nature.

🐾 Animal Welfare Impact

The foundation has contributed to the well-being of stray and injured animals by providing food, care, and support, fostering compassion and responsibility towards animals.

💼 Youth Empowerment Impact

By offering skill development programs, career guidance, and mentorship opportunities, NayePankh Foundation has empowered young people to build confidence and improve their future career prospects.

🤝 Community Development Impact

The foundation has strengthened community engagement by encouraging volunteerism and social responsibility, bringing people together to work towards meaningful social change.

🌟 Overall Impact

NayePankh Foundation has positively impacted society by promoting education, environmental conservation, animal welfare, and youth development, helping create a more inclusive, sustainable, and compassionate community.
